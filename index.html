CrystalsCollector!

Game Instructions:


A Magic Number will be given at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. This value is hidden until the player clicks a crystal. When the crystal is clicked, the player's score counter will update.


The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. The player loses if their score goes above the random number.


Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
